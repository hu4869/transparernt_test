Kimberly will present her research work at AMIA 2015 Annual Symposium

Congratulations to Kimberly Harris! She will be giving a podium presentation titled "A survey of social media for understanding patient-reported medication outcomes", at the AMIA Annual Symposium held in San Francisco in November. She conducted the research in the past year as a Graduate Research Assistant in the Health Informatics Lab, by working with Prof. Lixia Yao and PhD student, Mr. Boshu Ru. Harris completed her PSM degree in Health Informatics in May 2015 and is currently contracting full-time, as a Data Analyst, for Optum Technologies.
